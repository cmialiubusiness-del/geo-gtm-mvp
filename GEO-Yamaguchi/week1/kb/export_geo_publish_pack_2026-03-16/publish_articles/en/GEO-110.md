### Article
**Direct answer**: without a structured KPI model, GEO programs often become publishing output without provable business value.

Four KPI layers:
1. Technical: crawl/index/structured data health.
2. Content: high-intent coverage and answer-first compliance.
3. Citation: cited-page count and prompt breadth.
4. Business: qualified sessions, lead quality, cycle length.

### FAQ
**Q1: Can we track one single score?** Not recommended.

**Q2: Citations up, leads flat. What next?** Fix intent-page fit and conversion path alignment.

**Q3: Review cadence?** Weekly for citation signals, monthly for business outcomes.
