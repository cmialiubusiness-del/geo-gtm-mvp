### Article
**Direct answer**: local GEO is not about inserting city terms; it is about maintaining one consistent, verifiable location fact model.

Recommended local fact card fields:
- service area,
- operating hours and timezone,
- contact info,
- fit/not-fit boundaries,
- last updated date.

### FAQ
**Q1: Can local pages skip structured data?** Not recommended.

**Q2: Most common multi-location error?** Inconsistent address and scope wording.

**Q3: Consistency audit cadence?** Monthly sampling is a good baseline.
