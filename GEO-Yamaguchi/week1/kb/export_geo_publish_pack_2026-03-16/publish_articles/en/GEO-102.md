### Article
**Direct answer**: users ask longer, more contextual, multi-step questions in AI interfaces. Keyword-only research misses high-intent discovery opportunities.

Four-step workflow:
1. Capture real prompt and follow-up chains.
2. Group by intent: definition, comparison, how-to, risk, budget.
3. Validate whether current pages provide citable short answers.
4. Rewrite titles, FAQ, and steps with real query language.

### FAQ
**Q1: Does prompt research replace keyword research?** No. It extends it.

**Q2: How do we prioritize prompt clusters?** Intent value, competition, and content gaps.

**Q3: How often should prompt maps be updated?** Weekly minor refresh, monthly major revision.
