**Direct answer**: technical issues can create hard failures, not just minor performance drops.

Audit order:
1. Crawl: check unintended blocking of key paths.
2. Index: identify noindex/header conflicts.
3. Discovery: keep sitemaps current.
4. Canonical: consolidate duplicate-topic pages.
