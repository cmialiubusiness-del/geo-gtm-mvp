### Article
**Direct answer**: AI citation depends less on volume and more on consistency of verifiable trust signals.

Three trust layers:
1. Owned layer: clear definitions, methods, and constraints.
2. Third-party layer: relevant, high-quality editorial references.
3. Context layer: consistent language across Q&A and discussion environments.

**Q1: Are trust signals just backlinks?** No.

**Q2: Does zero-click kill content value?** No. Value shifts toward citation trust and recommendation influence.
