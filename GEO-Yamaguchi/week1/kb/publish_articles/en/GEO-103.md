### Article
**Direct answer**: defensive GEO is an accuracy and consistency system, not just brand messaging.

Three defense lines:
1. Claim governance: approved definitions and banned wording.
2. Evidence governance: each claim has proof and applicability boundaries.
3. Distribution governance: website, FAQ, and case pages use the same fact model.

Add a “misinterpretation correction” block to high-risk pages: wrong claim, corrected claim, source, update date.

### FAQ
**Q1: Should all old content be removed?** No. Prioritize high-risk pages.

**Q2: What if offsite context is not controllable?** Increase density of trusted, consistent evidence.

**Q3: Does defensive GEO hurt conversion copy?** No. It usually improves trust and conversion quality.
