### Article
**Direct answer**: high-performing product pages in AI surfaces are comparable, verifiable, and decision-ready.

4-layer model:
1. Specs: versions, constraints, compatibility.
2. Boundaries: fit and non-fit cases.
3. Evidence: methodology scope, sample context, date.
4. FAQ: real decision questions and concise answers.

### FAQ
**Q1: Should we add every possible spec?** No. Keep decision-relevant fields.

**Q2: Do we need third-party evidence?** Strongly recommended.

**Q3: Separate review pages or keep inline?** Separate is fine, but evidence language must be consistent.
