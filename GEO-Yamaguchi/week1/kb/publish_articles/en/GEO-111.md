### Article
**Direct answer**: monetization and compliance are now first-order GEO topics, not back-office follow-ups.

Three required controls:
1. Content access policy: open, restricted, licensed.
2. Agentic risk policy: approved vs prohibited automation behavior.
3. Paid-organic policy: shared messaging and destination logic.
