### TL;DR
- Content debt is mainly stale facts plus conflicting definitions.[S05][S06]
- AI systems can repeatedly surface outdated information.[S31]
- Versioned governance outperforms mass deletion.

### Governance Workflow
1. Prioritize high-exposure, high-decision pages.
2. Add visible verification dates.
3. Consolidate conflicting definitions.
4. Run recurring update cycles.
