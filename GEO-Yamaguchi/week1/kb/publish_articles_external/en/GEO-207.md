# GEO for Product Pages: A 4-Layer Design

## Article GEO-207

### TL;DR
- Product pages must support decisions, not only persuasion.[S20][S60]
- Use four layers: specs, boundaries, evidence, FAQ.
- “Pros-only” pages weaken long-term trust.[S35][S52]

### 4-Layer Design
1. Specs: versions, constraints, compatibility.
2. Boundaries: who it is and is not for.
3. Evidence: methodology, sample, date.
4. FAQ: real purchase and evaluation questions.
